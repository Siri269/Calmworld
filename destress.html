The 4-7-8 breathing activates the parasympathetic nervous system, which promotes relaxation and calms the body's "fight or flight" response.

Meditation

The five finger breathing is a simple mindfulness technique where you use your index finger to trace your other hands fingers while controlling your breath, breathing in as you trace up and out as you trace down.

Box breathing is a relaxation technique that involves inhaling, holding your breath, exhaling, and holding again, each for a count of 4 seconds. This sijmple process can help manage stress, anxiety, and improve focus by calmin the nervous system.
