  • Box Breathing

Box breathing is a relaxation technique that involves inhaling, holding your breath, exhaling, and holding again, each for a count of 4 seconds. This simple process can help manage stress, anxiety, and improve focus by calmin the nervous system

Meditation

The five finger breathing is a simple mindfulness technique where you use your index finger to trace your other hands fingers while controlling your breath, breathing in as you trace up and out as you trace down.

Diet

A balanced diet helps manage pre-test stress by providing stable energy, boosting mood-regulating neurotransmitters, and reducing inflammation. Eating complex carbohydrates, protein, and healthy fats prevents blood sugar crashes and provides sustained energy, while foods rich in B vitamins, vitamin C, and omega-3s support brain function and stress hormone regulation. Limiting caffeine and sugar also prevents jitteriness and heightened anxiety.
